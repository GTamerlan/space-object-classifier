This AI-powered Space Object Classifier was created as part of the MITES Semester at MIT, for the Machine Learning project course, a program that empowers high school students to explore science and engineering through hands-on learning and collaboration.

The system uses a pretrained model with custom-trained Network V2 Machine model and TensorFlow.js to identify celestial images from 4 classes, galaxies, comets, planets, and nebulas from uploaded photos. In the future we plan to add more. It also simulates a GPT-based vision model to provide comparison with our model and intelligent captions and detailed descriptions, creating the feeling of operating a futuristic space observatory.
